K-12 newcomer ELD curriculum

Brooklyn

A curriculum-first English Language Development platform for secondary newcomers, built around WIDA-aligned Objective Cards that render to print packets, projector routines, and device tasks.

Scope & Sequence 12-unit WIDA-aligned curriculum plan with delivery-agnostic structure. Curriculum Reference All 12 units — 48 objective cards, 144 leveled variants, every domain. Objective Card Prototype One authored object rendered as print and device learning experiences. Curriculum Schema The source model for units, domains, levels, checks, and render targets.
Independent alignment to the WIDA ELD Standards Framework. Brooklyn is not affiliated with, authorized by, or endorsed by WIDA or the University of Wisconsin.
